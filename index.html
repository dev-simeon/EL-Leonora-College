Welcome

EL-Leonora College was founded on the 5th of October 1999, with the secondary school(College) starting with 23 students.

At EL-Leonora College our mission is to provide quality education to as many children as possible to make them greater and responsible citizen for Nigeria in future.

Our aim is to provide the best academic program and learning environment possible for the social, emotional, and physical intellectual development of each child.
